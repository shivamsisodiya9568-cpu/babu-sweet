Our Story

A Legacy of Sweetness

Started in 1995 by Babu Gunda Ji, our sweet shop has been serving the community with authentic, handcrafted Indian sweets for over 25 years. What began as a small shop has grown into a beloved destination for sweet lovers.

Our secret lies in using traditional recipes, pure ingredients, and the patience that comes with decades of experience. Every sweet that leaves our kitchen carries the promise of quality and taste.
